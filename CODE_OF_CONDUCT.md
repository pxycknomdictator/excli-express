# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers of `@excli/express` pledge to make participation in our project and community a harassment-free experience for everyone — regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

---

## Our Standards

### ✅ Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Be respectful of differing viewpoints, experiences, and skill levels
- Give and receive constructive feedback gracefully
- Focus discussions on the work — ideas, code, and solutions
- Help newcomers get oriented — everyone starts somewhere
- Accept maintainer decisions on scope and direction, even when you disagree

### ❌ Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks — public or private
- Discriminatory language or jokes of any kind
- Publishing someone's private information (doxxing) without explicit consent
- Trolling, deliberately inflammatory comments, or derailing discussions
- Sustained disruption of issues, PRs, or community spaces
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional environment

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces — GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, commit messages, and any other communication directly related to this project.

It also applies when an individual is representing the project in public spaces, such as at events, meetups, or on social media.

---

## Reporting a Violation

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it **privately** to the maintainer:

📧 **[pxycknomdictator@gmail.com](mailto:pxycknomdictator@gmail.com)**

Please include in your report:
- A description of what happened
- Where it happened (link to the issue, PR, or comment if possible)
- Any relevant context or screenshots

All reports will be handled **confidentially**. The maintainer will acknowledge your report within **72 hours** and will work to resolve the situation as quickly as possible.

We will not retaliate against anyone who reports a violation in good faith.

---

## Enforcement

Maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject any contribution — comments, commits, code, issues, or otherwise — that violates this Code of Conduct.

Consequences for violations are determined based on severity and context:

| Level | Behavior | Consequence |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| 1 | Minor violation, likely unintentional | Private warning with explanation |
| 2 | Repeated minor violations or a single serious one | Formal warning with conditions |
| 3 | Serious violation or continued behavior after warning | Temporary suspension from the project |
| 4 | Severe violation or pattern of harmful behavior | Permanent ban from all project spaces |
